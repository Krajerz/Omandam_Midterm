Mt. Kitanglad

Mount Kitanglad is a dormant volcano in the Kitanglad Mountain Range in Mindanao's Bukidnon Province. It is the Philippines' fourth-highest peak, with an elevation of around 2,899 meters (9,511 feet). It lies between Malaybalay City and the municipalities of Lantapan, Impasugong, Sumilao, and Libona. It is home to one of the Philippines' last surviving rainforests.
